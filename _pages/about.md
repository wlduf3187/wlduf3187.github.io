I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at KAIST, expecting to receive my doctoral degree in August 2026. My research focuses on vehicle dynamics and control theory, with a particular emphasis on combination vehicle systems such as vehicle-trailer combinations, analyzed through handling diagrams, bifurcation theory, and modal analysis.

I am always interested in discussing vehicle dynamics, control systems, or related topics — feel free to reach out!
